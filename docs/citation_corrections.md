## Content-claim error (not just attribution)

- **Progress & Compress (Schwarz et al., ICML 2018) — attribution correct, but
  one claim is wrong.** The summary states retention / positive transfer
  *rises* with the number of prior tasks. The paper shows positive transfer is
  *maintained* (roughly flat: 127→129→125→129→128 with re-init), not
  increasing. The only rising curve in the paper is accuracy vs number of
  *re-visits* (Table 2), not vs number of prior tasks — likely the source of
  the conflation. **Fix the "retention rises with more tasks" framing.** The
  cyclic-revisit protocol claim is correct.
